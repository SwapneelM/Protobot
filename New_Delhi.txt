Seismology
New Delhi lies on several fault lines and thus experiences frequent earthquakes most of them of mild intensity There has however been a spike in the number of earthquakes in the last six years most notable being a 54 magnitude earthquake in 2015 with its epicentre in Nepal a 47magnitude earthquake on 25 November 2007 a 42magnitude earthquake on 7 September 2011 a 52magnitude earthquake on 5 March 2012 and a swarm of twelve earthquakes including four of magnitudes 25 28 31 and 33 on 12 November 2013
